Alchemist Game Documention

Here you find the descriptions of the ingredients and what they do, but no pictures. This is intentional!

Simple Ingredients

These only increase one property.
  • Heartflas
        Desc: Three green leaves tied together.
        Properties: Color+
  • Vasper
        Desc: A bundle of thin stalks, slightly dried.
        Properties: Viscosity+
  • Lucky Clover
        Desc: A four-leaf clover with mysterious properties.
        Properties: Smell+
  • Fluix
        Desc: A stalk with small blue flowers on it.
        Properties: Acidity+

Double Ingredients

These increase one property, and decrease another by the same ammount.
  • Yellow Gillion
        Desc: A plant with a large yellow flower.
        Properties: Color+, Smell-
  • Umbra Root
        Desc: A brownish-purple tuber.
        Properties: Acidity+, Viscosity-
  • Chinel Root
        Desc: A tough, reddish-brown root cluster.
        Properties: Smell+, Acidity-
  • Salvite
        Desc: A white mineral not unlike salt.
        Properties: Color-, Viscosity+

Triple Ingredients

These increase one property twice as much as normal, and decrease another two by the normal ammount.
  • Aqueos
        Desc: A bubbly fluid made from water.
        Properties: Smell-, Acidity-, Viscosity++
  • Mandrake
        Desc: An angery carrot-like root.
        Properties: Color-, Smell-, Acidity++
  • Red Arcane Shroom
        Desc: A spotted red mushroom.
        Properties: Color++, Acidity-, Viscosity-
  • Stink Cabbage Leaf
        Desc: A single green leaf, very smelly.
        Properties: Color-, Smell++, Viscosity-
