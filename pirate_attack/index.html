Pirate Attack

Use A and D to move, SPACE or W to jump, and click to attack. You cannot hold down attack.
  • Originality: Q: What is original about your game?
                        A: Good question. At this stage, its not much to look at. And there isn't a whole lot "original" with it, but, compared to Phaser templates, it has moving platforms and enemies to kill.
  • Technical Merit: Q: What is interesting about your game technology?
                        A: Unfortunately, again, not much. I tried to work with some better physics systems to provide smooth speedup and slowdown, but that caused a number of problems, including falling through platforms and getting launched sideways by vertical collisions. I ended up scrapping the entire thing, as it had so many bugs.
  • Prototype Postmortem: Q: What did you learn from this prototype? What was the easiest or hardest part of making it?
                        A: The primary thing I learned is that Phaser3 is a piece of junk. Its physics support is ... shoddy. At best. It does not have ANY way to get the velocity of a body. Very simple thing to do, and very nesecary. And there is no way to do it. This makes absolutely no sense. The other main thing I learned is that is is selectively buggy. There are animation bugs that don't make any sense. These two issues consumed almost all my time for this project, and guess what? Neither were solved, and not from lack of trying. One I had to give up the uniqueness of my game to get somthing playable(physics), the other just never got solved (animations).
